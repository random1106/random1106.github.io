My curriculum vitae.

Research interests: Probability theory, particularly the asymptotic behaviors of large interacting particle systems, and the interplay among partial differential equations (PDEs), stochastic PDEs, and integrable probability.

Before joining University of Chicago, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Simons Laufer Mathematical Sciences Institute (2021 Aug–2021 Dec). I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics at Columbia University in 2021. I obtained my B.S. in Mathematics at Tsinghua University in 2016.

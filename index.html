My curriculum vitae.

Research interests: Probability theory, particularly the asymptotic behaviors of interacting particle systems, and the interplay among PDEs, stochastic PDEs, and integrable probability.

Before moving to industry, I was a Kruskal Instructor of of Department of Statistics, University of Chicago . Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Mathematical Sciences Research Institute. I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics at Columbia University in 2021. I obtained my B.S. in Mathematics at Tsinghua University in 2016.
